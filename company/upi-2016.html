Invisible Giant

The press release that quietly launched the world's biggest payment rail

44 banks in 2016. 703 today.

  • 703banks now run on UPI - India's public payment rail.Source: NPCI; PIB, 2026.
  • 49%of the world's real-time payments now move on this one rail.Source: IMF, 2025.

The Global South does not just adopt infrastructure. It writes the standard the rest of the world copies.

On 11 April 2016, a press release announced a payments pilot with 44 banks. Almost nobody read it.

Ten years on, India's UPI connects 703 banks and carries close to half of the world's real-time payment volume, built not as a company but as public infrastructure any bank or app can plug into.

The decision that mattered: make the rail a public good, not a private moat.

The Global South does not just adopt infrastructure. It writes the standard the rest of the world copies.
