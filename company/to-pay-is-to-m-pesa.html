  • More than thirty million Kenyans, one wallet.On a network of agents that reaches corners the banks never did.

Some products get used. This one became a verb.

In Kenya, paying someone has a name, and the name is M-Pesa. More than thirty million people move money on it, through agents who reach corners the banks never did - no smartphone, no branch, no minimum balance required.

When the rail is the bank, the language changes with it.
