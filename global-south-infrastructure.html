What capsrow tracks

60 verified, people-scale numbers on the infrastructure the Global South is building - AI, financial, and energy systems - each linked to its primary source. capsrow counts infrastructure in people, products, and capacity, never in valuations.

By capsrow · Updated July 2026

Method. Every row is a single verified people-scale number - customers, households, units, megawatts, GPUs, languages - drawn from a primary disclosure (a company release, regulator, or official dataset). capsrow excludes valuations, funding, and revenue by design. This is a curated coverage list, not an analytic dataset: the rows measure different things and are meant to be read, not summed. Download the full list (CSV).
